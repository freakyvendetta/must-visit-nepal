sagarmatha national park

7 days Meduim Treehouse trek

Day 1: Arrival in Lukla

Embark on your Sagarmatha National Park adventure with a thrilling flight from Kathmandu to Lukla. The short and scenic journey sets the tone for the trek ahead. Upon landing, commence your trek to Phakding, a picturesque village situated at 2,610 meters above sea level. This initial leg offers a gentle introduction to the trekking experience.

Day 2: Phakding to Namche Bazaar

Rise early and continue your trek, crossing suspension bridges and enjoying the lush landscapes. The trail leads to Namche Bazaar, a bustling Sherpa town at an altitude of 3,440 meters. Spend the day acclimatizing, exploring the local market, and soaking in the panoramic views of the surrounding peaks.

Day 3: Namche Bazaar to Tengboche

Trek from Namche Bazaar to Tengboche at 3,870 meters. The journey offers stunning views of Everest, Lhotse, and Ama Dablam. Explore the famous Tengboche Monastery, a cultural highlight of the trek, and revel in the serene mountainous atmosphere.

Day 4: Tengboche to Dingboche

Continue your ascent to Dingboche at 4,410 meters. The trek provides breathtaking views of Everest and neighboring peaks. Upon reaching Dingboche, take in the beauty of the landscape and prepare for the challenges of higher altitudes.

Day 5: Acclimatization Day in Dingboche

This day is dedicated to acclimatization. Embark on a short hike to Nangkartshang Peak to help your body adjust to the increasing altitude. Explore Dingboche, interact with locals, and savor the unique Sherpa culture.

Day 6: Dingboche to Lobuche

Resume your trek from Dingboche to Lobuche, situated at 4,940 meters. Traverse through Thukla Pass, paying homage to the Everest Memorial along the way. The route offers glimpses of the challenging yet awe-inspiring terrain.

Day 7: Lobuche to Gorak Shep and Everest Base Camp

The final stretch takes you from Lobuche to Gorak Shep at 5,164 meters. From Gorak Shep, embark on a memorable hike to Everest Base Camp at 5,364 meters, surrounded by the world's highest peaks. After soaking in the moment, return to Gorak Shep for a well-deserved rest and reflection on the incredible journey through the heart of Sagarmatha National Park.
